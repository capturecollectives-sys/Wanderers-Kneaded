Who we are

Mobile pizzeria. City regular. Event machine.

Wanderers Kneaded began with the simple ambition to cook honestly, serve generously and bring proper wood-fired pizza into Edinburgh's daily rhythm. Today the vans are recognised for fast service, characterful menus, and the kind of dough people cross town for.

It is casual food delivered with serious standards: 72-hour style fermentation, hand-stretched bases, bright toppings, reliable teams and vans that can feel equally at home beside The Meadows or inside a major event plan.
